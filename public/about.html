Our Story

At ReferralTrack, we believe in the power of word-of-mouth marketing. We're on a mission to help businesses harness the potential of their existing customers to drive growth through smart, AI-powered referral programs.

Who We Are

Founded in 2021, ReferralTrack was born out of a simple observation: traditional customer acquisition methods were becoming increasingly expensive and less effective, while the most valuable customers often came through referrals.

Our team of marketing experts, data scientists, and software engineers came together to build a platform that makes it easy for any business to create, manage, and optimize referral programs that actually convert.

Unlike other referral platforms, we use advanced AI to continuously analyze and improve your campaigns, ensuring you get the maximum return on investment. Our platform helps you identify your most influential customers, personalize incentives, and track every aspect of your referral program's performance.
